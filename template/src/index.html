Inside the template

Your entire narrative inside src/.

  • /src/index.html – hero, feature grid, trust indicators.
  • /src/pages/ – optional extra pages wired to HtmlWebpack.
  • /src/scss/ – tokens, mixins, and layout partials with meaningful naming.
  • /src/js/app.js – animation hooks, sample interactions, and space for your scripts.
